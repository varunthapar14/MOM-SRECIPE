Featured Dishes

Canadian Butter Tarts

Canadian Butter Tarts are a beloved traditional dessert that hails from Canada. These tarts feature a flaky, buttery pastry shell filled with a sweet, gooey mixture of butter, sugar, and eggs.

Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Chocolate Avocado Mousse is a delicious and healthy dessert that combines the rich creaminess of ripe avocados with the deep, indulgent flavor of dark chocolate. This mousse is incredibly smooth and velvety, offering a guilt-free alternative.

Egg Drop Soup

Egg Drop Soup, also known as Egg Flower Soup, is a simple and comforting Chinese soup that features beaten eggs gently poured into hot broth, creating delicate, wispy strands. The broth is typically flavored with chicken or vegetable stock.

Koshari

Koshari is a popular Egyptian dish known for its hearty and satisfying combination of ingredients. It typically consists of layers of rice, lentils, and pasta, topped with a flavorful tomato sauce and crispy fried onions.

Lancashire hotpot

Lancashire Hotpot is a traditional English dish originating from the county of Lancashire. It is a hearty and comforting casserole-style dish consisting of layers of lamb or beef, onions, and potatoes, cooked slowly to tender perfection.

Poutine

Poutine is a beloved Canadian dish that originated in the province of Quebec. It consists of crispy French fries topped with fresh cheese curds and smothered in hot gravy.
